Regional Medical Education Conference

Region V is home to eighteen medical and pre-medical student chapters, representing approximately 600 members throughout the states of Indiana, Michigan, and Ohio. Our yearly Regional Conference serves as a platform for pre-medical students, medical students, residents, and attendings to network, to learn new skills, to share our research, and to commit to community service. This year the conference will be hosted by Michigan State University College of Human Medicine.

SNMA Mission Statement

Commitment to supporting current and future underrepresented minority medical students
Addressing the needs of underserved communities
And increasing the number of clinically excellent, culturally competent and socially conscious physicians

Research & Community Engagement Presentations

Please join our Research & Community Engagement Symposium! The set up this year will be similar to those in the past; each student will have 8 minutes to present 3 - 6 PowerPoint slides about their research/project. We welcome published and unpublished work as well as volunteer and community engagement projects that you have participated in that aligns with SNMA’s mission. This is a great opportunity to practice presenting and will be a noteworthy addition to your CV. Deadline for applications is October 6th. Please fill out this Google Form with the information about what you want to present. You will be hearing from Ranuka Sinniah with a follow up and details about presenting.

Educational Partners

Welcome Educational Partner! We would like to plan an event that represents the best of the college. It is very important we demonstrate what makes us special and unique. A part of this is to showcase the wonderful support that we receive from our departments, units, and communities. We hope that you will seriously consider joining our efforts and supporting this conference as an official Educational Partner. We have outlined several levels of support for our partners which are described on the registration page (coming soon). Exhibitors

The registration deadline for exhibition tables is August 31, 2017 at 11:59PM. Space is limited so please reserve your table as soon as possible. We need you to sign up as a standard or gold exhibitor!
